Past

Secret Santa

A fun project I worked on a while ago is a "Secret Santa for Big Families" script. I have a large extended family that likes to play Secret Santa during Christmas, but it has always been a pain to make sure that no one is the Secret Santa for someone in their immediate family. After all, where's the fun in that? The Python script I wrote solves this problem and speeds up the process by eliminating the step of drawing slips of paper out of a hat. The code is available on GitHub.
